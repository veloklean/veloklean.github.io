Veloklean — Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect data for Customers, Cleaners & Representatives.

Privacy

We respect your privacy

This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how we use it, and your rights. Applies to Customers, Cleaners, and Reps engaging with Veloklean.

1

Data We Collect

Types of personal & non-personal data

We collect data necessary to provide services, communicate, and comply with legal obligations. This includes:

  • Customers: name, phone, email, address, booking details, payment tokens (via payment gateway), feedback and photos provided for service disputes.
  • Cleaners: name, contact, banking/payment details, ID/verification documents, availability, service records, background-check results (if applicable).
  • Representatives (Reps): name, contact, work history, communications records, performance data and timesheets.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP addresses, device/browser info, pages visited, ad interaction, cookies and analytics data.
  • Third-Party Data: data received from partners (e.g., payment processors, background-check providers) necessary to deliver services.
2

How We Use Your Data

Purpose & lawful basis

  • Service Delivery: to schedule, assign, and perform cleanings; share relevant contact and job details with assigned cleaners and reps.
  • Payments & Billing: to process payments, issue invoices, reconcile payouts, and prevent fraud via trusted payment processors.
  • Communications: to send booking confirmations, reminders, receipts, support messages and service updates (email, SMS, WhatsApp).
  • Quality & Safety: to verify cleaner eligibility, perform background checks if required, and investigate incidents or complaints.
  • Marketing: to send service offers and updates to customers who opt-in. You can opt-out anytime.
  • Legal & Compliance: to comply with laws, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights and users.

We only use personal data for the purpose it was collected or where consent or a legal basis exists.

3

Sharing & Third Parties

Who we may share data with

  • Assigned Cleaners & Reps: receive only job-relevant data (client name, address, phone, service details).
  • Payment Processors: (Stripe, PayPal, Instamojo or equivalents) to securely process payments; we do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Background Check Providers: when required for cleaner onboarding (with explicit consent).
  • Analytics & Ads Partners: (Meta, Google) to analyze ad performance and improve service delivery—data is shared per provider policies.
  • Legal & Compliance: when required by law, court order or to protect our rights or users.

We require third parties to follow industry-standard security practices and prohibit unauthorized reuse of personal data.

4

Cookies & Tracking

How we use cookies & similar tech

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our site, analyze usage, show personalized ads, and improve user experience. You can control cookie preferences via your browser settings. For ad opt-outs, visit your ad platform settings (Google, Meta).

5

Data Retention

How long we keep data

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and for legitimate business purposes. Typical retention periods:

  • Booking records & invoices: retained for 7 years for accounting and tax purposes.
  • Cleaner onboarding records: retained while active and for a reasonable period after offboarding.
  • Marketing consents: until you opt-out or withdraw consent.
6

Security

How we protect your data

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data. Examples include encrypted connections (HTTPS), restricted access, and secure cloud storage. However no system is 100% secure—report any suspected breach immediately to team@veloklean.services.

7

Your Rights

Access, correction, deletion & objections

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion where we have no legal obligation to retain it (subject to verification).
  • Objection & Restriction: You can object to certain processing (e.g., direct marketing).

To exercise these rights, contact team@veloklean.ca. We may need to verify identity before fulfilling requests.

8

International Transfers

If data moves across borders

Veloklean may transfer data to service providers and partners located outside your country (for example, payment processors or hosting providers). We take steps to ensure adequate protection, such as standard contractual clauses or relying on processors with strong security standards.

9

Changes to this Policy

How we notify updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will note the “Last updated” date below and notify active users of material changes by email when required.

10

Contact & Data Requests

How to reach us

For questions, access requests, or privacy concerns contact:

Email: team@veloklean.ca

We aim to respond within 5 business days.
