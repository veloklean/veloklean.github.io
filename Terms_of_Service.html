Customers — Service Terms

Bookings, payments, cancellations, expectations

Service & Bookings: Veloklean provides cleaning services as described at booking time. Customers must provide accurate address and access details. Bookings are confirmed once payment is processed (or deposit received) and a confirmation is shared.

  • Payment: Customers pay using the methods provided (Stripe, PayPal, Instamojo or agreed gateway). Payment policies are shown at checkout.

Note: Veloklean is a service provider and coordinates cleaners. Cleaners are independent contractors unless otherwise agreed.

Cleaners — Engagement Terms

Status, pay, standards, confidentiality

Independent Contractor: Cleaners engaged by Veloklean act as independent contractors. Cleaners are responsible for their own taxes, insurance (if applicable), equipment and transport unless otherwise agreed.

  • Compensation: Cleaners receive a stated percentage of the service fee (for example, 55%). Payout schedules are weekly or as agreed and subject to reconciliation with completed jobs and client confirmation.
  • Standards: Cleaners must follow Veloklean's quality checklist. Repeated service failures or client complaints may lead to suspension.
  • Scheduling & Assignment: Jobs are assigned automatically by the Veloklean system based on location, availability, and daily capacity. Cleaners must confirm assignments and arrive on time.
  • Equipment & Safety: Cleaners must use the agreed cleaning supplies and follow safety procedures. For specialized sites (clinics, restaurants) additional PPE or training may be required.
  • Confidentiality & Client Data: Cleaners must not share client contact details outside of Veloklean channels or solicit clients for direct work.

Veloklean provides onboarding materials and may require identification and background checks depending on market rules.

Representatives — Booking & Sales Terms

Role, pay, performance and data rules

Role: Representatives (reps) contact leads, confirm bookings, send payment links, and update Veloklean systems with client details. Reps may work on a hybrid model (hourly + per booking incentive) as per agreement.

  • Compensation: Pay terms for reps are detailed in individual agreements (e.g., per-booking fee, hourly pay, performance bonuses). Payments are processed weekly after verification of completed bookings.
  • Data Handling: Reps receive lead data for business purposes only. Reps must not reuse, resell or share lead lists externally. Any breach will lead to termination and potential legal action.
  • Verification Process: Reps must collect and confirm client payment where required before assigning a cleaner. Veloklean may require photo proof, payment receipts, or post-service confirmations.
  • Quality & Conduct: Reps must represent Veloklean professionally. Any misrepresentation or unauthorized promises may result in immediate termination.

Reps are encouraged to use the Veloklean onboarding guide and scripts for consistent client experience.
