Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I start?

After submitting your application, our onboarding team typically reviews it within 48 hours. Once approved, you complete a quick training module and can start accepting jobs right away!

Do I need prior experience?

Experience in residential or commercial cleaning is preferred but **not mandatory**. We provide comprehensive training on Veloklean standards and techniques.

How will I get paid?

Payments are processed weekly or monthly via secure bank transfer or e-wallet. You earn up to 55% commission on every completed job.

Can I choose my own hours?

Yes, complete flexibility! You use the Veloklean app to set your availability, choose the areas you serve, and accept only the jobs that fit your schedule.
